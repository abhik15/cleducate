Roles
A School for parents and students alike
At Indus we help parents and children understand and learn from each other. You are an integral part of the learning process. You are our partners. We share with you everything that happens here, including our approach to teaching e.g. fractions and Newton’s laws, so that you may be in
a position to help your child.

 We do not believe in overloading children with homework. At the most, there will be occasional activities that we recommend you do at home with your child. You may need to help the child make a family tree, tell him the names of her great grand parents and trace the tree as far back as you can. You may want to come into school and share your knowledge and experiences from your area of work.

Join us in this journey of Infinity!
Feel free to come visit us. Have a stroll and meet some of our mentors. Remember, you are not a customer to Indus — you are our partner. What we can achieve together is as infinite as the possibilities your child has.
