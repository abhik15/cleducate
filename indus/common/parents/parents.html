Parents
Parenting is the most important job you will ever hold. However there is no school that can teach you to be a better parent. It is commonly assumed that, being a parent, you know everything about raising children. In our interactions with you, we work under no such assumptions.

Your role
If you are one of those people who feel that the school will take care of everything, stop right there. We won't because we can't. Learning for children begins when unlearning for adults takes place. Both parents and educators hold the stakes for education. We are in this together. Parent enabling and education is integral to the school's core ideology, that of -

"Helping children realize their potential and make their dreams come true".

How can you be involved in your child’s education? What can you do in this scheme of infinite possibilities?

You will by now have seen that the organic structure of Indus consists of the mentor, the parent and the child. You would also have understood that the child is at the centre of it all but you, the parent are an equal partner along with the school in your child’s education and growth. Your role is as crucial if not more than the school’s. Feel free to browse and contact us any time with any queries. You may write to Col K.K. Mowar, Executive Director at kk.mowar@clef.in.
