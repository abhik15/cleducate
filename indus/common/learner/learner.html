The Learner
The learner is at the heart of everything that happens in Indus. If we drew a diagram, our approach to learning would look something like this…

 

As you can see, it’s all about the child. And so is the Indus philosophy and curriculum — completely child-centric. In other words, it’s the learner’s participation, experience, voice
and opinions that matter the most. Our mission as parents and mentors is simply to enable her
to realise her potential and achieve her dreams.

The Indus Learner
Indus facilitates learning through The Spirit of Inquiry. The Indus learner realizes true potential and turns a life dream into a living reality by inculcating a few simple basic tenets of learning:
  • Learn by your own terms
  • Learn by doing
  • Take play as seriously as study
  • Understand the world by helping and reaching out to the community
  • Take control of and know thy self inorder to find a purpose in life
These and ofcourse the art of learning for living are at the heart of the Indus curriculum.
