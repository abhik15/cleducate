Multiple Intelligence
Learning is not all about how smart you are but “HOW” you are smart! The theory of Multiple Intelligence (MI theory) states that each and every one of us explores the world and learns through the senses. In other words, we each have a unique learning style or intelligence. According to the MI theory, the different ways we learn can be categorized under eight different styles. We use a mix of these with one or two being predominant and best suited for a unique way of learning.

At work at Indus
The Indus way of learning accommodates all of these eight intelligences. Music, the arts and craft and sport are not ‘extra’ or co-curricular to us. They are the soul of our learning process.

Ignited Minds
Our very own Talent Discovery and Career Management program is designed and built upon the MI theory. Ignited Minds recognizes the right of every single child to discover her true potential, make those dreams take shape and find a career path to the same.
