Theory
Each child is unique
Each child is multitalented and has infinite potential. Some children have beautiful voices, some are great with their hands, and some find their spirit on the cricket filed. Some spend hours trying to solve complex problems in physics. They are all different but what is common is that they are each born with a special natural talent, unique only to the self. That one thing is their true spirit.

As a wise man once said…do what you are good at and you will be great at what you do…

 The world has come a long way. The career choices available today are even more numerous than they were when Sachin or Patricia made their fortunes. Your child need not only be a doctor or an engineer anymore. Many new careers – including the one your child is made for - are within reach today.

Just need opportunity
All your child needs is an opportunity to engage with the world and find out what truly fascinates her. It’s called ‘finding the true calling’. It comes with knowing the self. And only when that happens can she know what she is truly capable of.

Multiple Intelligence
The Indus way of learning accommodates all of the eight Multiple Intelligences as propounded by Gardner. Music, the arts and craft and sport are not ‘extra’ or co-curricular to us. MI is the soul of our learning process.

The important thing to remember …
At this moment in your homes, live little Vikram Seths, Tendulkars, and Zakir Hussains waiting to be discovered.
