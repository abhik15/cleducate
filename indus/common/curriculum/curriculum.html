Curriculum
‘Out of syllabus’
Such is the story of life. And our curriculum is no different. It strives to bring the outside world inside by explaining complex things in the simplest possible way and making a connection with our lives so that it can be easily understood. A clear understanding gives rise to sheer joy. This joy makes each learner at Indus… a learner for life.

The word 'curriculum' is much confused in meaning to the word 'syllabus'. At Indus World School, curriculum is the way of life, and is not restricted to our learning methods. It is an inclusion of academic pursuit along with all activities inside the classroom and outside it.
