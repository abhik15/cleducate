Ignited Minds
Realizing potential
What will your child grow up to be? What career choice will she make? How …?
It’s quite natural for parents to engage in such questions regarding their children’s
future. It’s never is too early to start thinking about your child’s future. But be careful.
Sometimes, in all our worry, we start enforcing our expectations on the children.

Ignited Minds
Where every single child has the right to infinite opportunities to discover her true potential,
where dreams take shape, where the path to those dreams becomes clear. Ignited Minds is Indus World School’s talent discovery and career management program operating under
the three wings of Arts, Sports and Academia. It is the talent incubation lab set up under the
aegis of Nalanda Foundation (NF) with an objective to discover talent and nurture them into success stories. Ignited Minds picks up talent in diverse areas such as music, art, drama, dance, games and sports, science, literature etc at the earliest possible stage using scientific techniques.

One of the key objectives of Ignited Minds is to ensure that children focus on their skill development and performance while their parents focus on offering a supportive environment at home. Ignited Minds pitch in with every kind of support to make careers possible, such as coaching, mentoring by competent people, financial support beyond parents' abilities and exposure to the outside world.

 Ignited Minds as of today focuses on three broad areas:
  • Ignited Minds - Sports
  • Ignited Minds - Arts
  • Ignited Minds - Academia
