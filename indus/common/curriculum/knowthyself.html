Know thy self
Who are you?
Every child has a different set of Aptitude, Personality and Interests. Also each child has a different Learning Style as propounded in Gardener's theory of Multiple Intelligence. We believe that every type of endeavour - academic and non-academic - must be recognised and encouraged. The child must be encouraged to pursue her interests with complete freedom, whatever they might be.

It is only with a healthy mix of these that learning becomes a joy and fosters an inquisitive personality. The recognition of multiple intelligence respects the basic difference in us all and helps to choose a career path that suits your API (aptitude-interest-personality).
