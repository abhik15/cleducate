About Us

The Indus World School and it's sister concern the Ananda School are promoted by Nalanda Foundation (NF) - an educational society created with the sole aim of providing quality education comparable to the best in the world. NF was co-founded by a passionate group of educators who aim to contribute directly to society and nation building. This eclectic group of professionals are from various fields: Technology, Management, Design, etc. and are alumni from leading institutes in the country viz. IITs, IIMs and NID. They share a common vision of 'a place of learning with a difference' - one that is futuristic and nurtures tomorrow's leaders.

A brief profile of some of the key people at Nalanda Foundation (NF), the group behind Indus World School:

Satya Narayanan R, the chairman and chief mentor at NF, is a renowned speaker in Indian academic circles. He has trained thousands of students, teachers and corporate executives and is, according to many sections of the media, the moving force behind several pioneering initiatives in the Asian education market.

Col. Krish Mowar is executive director of NF. During his tenure as the Principal Director of the Army Welfare Education Society, he managed 116 schools and 8 professional colleges with more than 150,000 students. He tripled admissions there and added seven new colleges to that list, thus proving that academic excellence is just a matter of dedication.

R Shiva Kumar is an alumnus of IIT and IIM and has more than a decade of experience teaching mathematics and science for engineering and medical entrance examinations. His ultimate goal in Indus World School is to make science easy and understandable at a primary school level.

R Sreenivasan holds an M Tech and an MBA from IIM. His mission involves facilitating the children to use their right brain. Brings in confluence of theatre and art into all levels of school curricula. more...

Gautam Puri, an engineer and an MBA from IIM, runs a multi-crore-education company (one of Asia’s largest). His goal in Indus World School is to completely redefine the way mathematics is taught at school.

Sujit Bhattacharyya, also an IIT-IIM alumnus, brings a passion for the right use of technology combined with a love for education into Indus World School. His hands-on experience in various Silicon Valley start-ups backs him in his endeavours.

Similar are the stories and motivations for others like Nikhil Mahajan, Sharad Awasthi, Arindam Lahiri, Sanjay Shivnani, Vijay Kumar, Nandini Sood...

To know more about this ‘visionary’ group, feel free to visit the Indus World School and speak with a parent-advisor.
