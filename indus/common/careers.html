Careers at Indus World School

At Indus World School we do not believe in "teaching" or teachers… only 'facilitators'

Do you...
Believe that each child is unique, has unlimited potential and more importantly,
Believe that you have what it takes to bring out that potential?

Do you...
Believe in the tremendous joy of learning and teaching?
Absolutely adore being in the company of children, before, during and after school hours?

If you possess the generous joy of learning …then we would like to meet with you…

What we offer...
We believe in helping each member of the Indus World School family realize their full potential and make their dreams come true. We support initiative, particularly those pertaining to research and development in education. If your personal goals and those of the institution are aligned, working at Indus World School will be an enriching and rewarding experience. Needless to say, monetary numeration is the best in the industry and can even be better for deserving candidates.

The process
  • Skill evaluation
  • Psychometric test
  • "Live" demo class
  • Outbound training program (four days)
