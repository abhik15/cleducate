At Indus, it's all work and all play, all day round. The fine line between the two (work and play) has been eradicated. Being it theatre, dance, drama, sport, community service or music, it all happens here. Keep in mind, at Indus World School, work is play and vice versa. Location is irrelevant in our scheme of things. It doesn't matter whether you are inside the physical classroom or on the playing field or sitting under the oak tree on the Indus Estate, work and play happens at all time.
