Indus facilitates "The Spirit of Inquiry". So that each child can realize her/his true potential and turn a life dream into a living reality. We do this by enabling the child to:
  • Learn on her own terms
  • Learn by doing
  • Take play as seriously as study
  • Understand the world by helping and reaching out to the community
  • Take control of and know thy self inorder to find a purpose in life
