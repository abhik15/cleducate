Ignited Minds
Ignited Minds is the talent incubation lab set up under the aegis of CLEF with an objective to discover talent and nurture them into success stories. Ignited Minds would pick talent in diverse areas such as music, art, drama, dance, games and sports, science, literature etc at the earliest possible stage using scientific techniques.

One of the key objectives of Ignited Minds is to ensure that the children focus on their skill development and performance, their parents focus on offering a supportive environment for the child at home. Ignited Minds will pitch in with every other kind of support such as coaching, mentoring by competent people, financial support beyond parents' abilities, exposure to the outside world.

Ignited Minds begins with focus on three broad areas:
  • Ignited Minds - Sports
  • Ignited Minds - Arts
  • Ignited Minds - Academia
