Ignited Minds Sports
 This is a privately funded programme under the aegis of Ignited Minds to bridge the gap in sport’s talent development pathway between junior representative sport and world-class levels for our most talented 14 to 20 year-old sportspeople. It represents a unique partnership between sport and higher and further education and is structured to help aspiring young sportspeople maintain a sensible balance between academic life or employment whilst training and competing as a performance athlete.

‘Ignited Minds Sports’ is a unique project, under the Ignited Minds initiative that seeks to rope in government and non-government organizations, sporting personalities, coaching institutions and Universities, business organizations to help in the goal of ‘talent discovery and career managemement’ for young talented athletes.

Firstly, Ignited Minds Sports will distribute the Ignited Minds money to the CLEF institutions to fund the services being delivered to the athletes,

Secondly, it will monitor how this money is spent

Thirdly, it will develop the commercial potential of Ignited Minds to grow the funds available to support the athletes in their career pursuit.

If you'd like to know more about Ignited Minds Sports, then please feel free to contact Col K.K. Mowar, Executive Director at kk.mowar@clef.in.


‘Sport and Study’ helps talented young sportspeople make informed choices about their University and college applications. This exciting new service aims to offer you, the student, a valuable insight into the opportunities available to you as you make one of the biggest decisions of your life. Which university to go to, which best matches your high performance sporting skills. We have a range of information that will help make your choice easier. This program is designed for University and college applicants who are also talented athletes - either qualifying for a Ignited Minds bursary or receiving funding as part of the World Class Performance Plan.
