Learning to Unlearn

Education has changed
The world around us has evolved far from what it used to be when we were in school. Are you, the parent in touch with it? The needs and demands of young children are far different from our time. Are you in tune with them? The pressures on not just children but also on parents are more than ever. Are you up to it?

We firmly believe and know that that you have the best intentions for your child. However, there is research that suggests that even a parent who has the best academic and professional background suffers from handicaps that require professional guidance to overcome. This may include the lack of Accurate and current information

The first step
It's time for us - you the parent and we the educators to get oriented in the basics of overall child development. You are equal stakeholders in your child's future therefore active participation from your end is required. Indus will conduct a series of ongoing workshops and Parent Labs on subjects ranging from How to make Math simpler to the basics of Child psychology. We believe that you will not only have great fun in these workshops but also equip yourself with better parenting skills as you go along.
