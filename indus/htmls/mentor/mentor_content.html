Mentor

No Teaching
The ability to Innovate is a key requirement for any teacher. We do not believe in teaching or teachers. At CLEF, we prefer the term "mentors/facilitators". We are facilitators and will only create an environment where the child is compelled (on her own) to think, ask questions, inquire, ask more questions and draw her own conclusions. We are only about an atmosphere to learn and grow.

Only Questioning
Teachers merely answer questions. But a facilitator is one who provokes you to ask even more questions. CLEF firmly holds that, NEVER discouraging a question is what makes the difference between a good teacher and a great facilitator.
