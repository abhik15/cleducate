Who are you learner?
Every child has a different Aptitude-Personality-Interest quotient. Also each child has a different Learning Style as propounded in Gardener's theory of Multiple Intelligence. We believe that every type of endeavour must be recognised and encouraged - academic and non-academic. The child must be encouraged to pursue her interests with complete freedom, whatever those interests may be.

It is only with a healthy mix of these that learning becomes a joy and fosters an inquisitive personality. The recognition of multiple intelligence respects the basic difference in us all and helps to choose a career path that suits your (API) aptitude-interest-personality quotient.
