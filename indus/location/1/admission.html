Dear Parent,
Welcome to Indus World School. Admissions are currently open for Classes 1 to Class 7.

Your child will be selected for admission subject to the availability of seats and the fulfilling of the admission norms. Generally speaking, these norms are based on the distance that your child will have to commute from home to school.

The School management would like to interact with both parents and the child to enable us to understand each others aspirations and goals. We prefer to interact in an informal environment at your home and will be in touch to fix a time suitable to you.

Registration fee is Rupees 500.00 (Five Hundred only) which may be paid by cash. You will be handed over a brochure on payment of registration fee. Kindly fill in the registration form and mail or deposit it at our city or site office.

After we receive your form and registration fee, you will receive from us the school brochure and a registration number, which you will need to quote in your subsequent interactions with us.

A registration number will be generated and communicated to you which maybe referred in all further communication.

Should you need any clarification, please call any of the following personnel of the School Management:
