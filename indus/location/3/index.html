Located in Sai Kripa Colony in the proximity of Bombay Hospital, Indore, Indus World School is offering admission for Classes 1 to 6 in the academic year 2006-07. The first session commenced from July 2006.
  - Admissions for academic year 2007- 2008 (nursery to class 6th) are now open.

Key features of the school
  • Teacher to student Ratio 1:12
  • Subject Specific learning areas
  • Out door learning areas
  • Special rooms and faculty for art,
    • Music
    • Dance
    • Craft
  • Wading pools
  • Puppet room
  • Play room
  • Science museum
  • Amphitheater
  • Lunch for the children
  • No heavy bags / individual cupboards
  • School transport
